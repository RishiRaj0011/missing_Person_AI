How Our Technology Works

Advanced AI algorithms working around the clock to find missing persons

1. Upload Photos

Provide clear, recent photos of the missing person from multiple angles to train our AI system for optimal recognition accuracy.

2. Add Search Videos

Upload surveillance footage, crowd videos, or any video content where the person might appear for comprehensive analysis.

3. AI Analysis

Our advanced facial recognition AI analyzes the videos and identifies potential matches with industry-leading accuracy.

4. Get Results

Receive detailed results with timestamps, confidence scores, and thumbnail images of potential matches within minutes.
